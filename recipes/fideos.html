Rice noodles with stir-fried tofu and bell pepper

ingredients

  • Rice noodles ------- 120 gr
  • firm tofu ---------- 200 gr
  • Red pepper ------------------- 1
  • Fresh Ginger ---------- 1 piece
  • Soy sauce ---------- 15 ml
  • Ground curry ---------------- 1/2 cdtas
  • Granulated garlic ------------- 1/4 cdtas
  • Ground turmeric ----------------- 1 cdtas
  • Ground black pepper
  • Salt
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro

preparation

  1. Discard the liquid from the tofu and drain well. Wrap in several layers of kitchen paper and leave to act for at least 15 minutes with a weight on top. Cut into bite-size cubes. Heat a little oil in a pan and brown the tofu on all sides. Withdraw.
  2. Cook the rice noodles in boiling water with a little salt for about three minutes, following the instructions on the package. Drain and rinse under cold water, loosening them a bit with a fork. Reserve.
  3. Grate or finely chop the ginger. Cut the pepper into thin strips. Sauté both ingredients in the same skillet over high heat for two minutes. Salt and pepper, add the soy sauce and spices. Skip 5 minutes. Return the tofu, stir and add the noodles. Mix everything well until they are integrated. Serve with chopped parsley.
