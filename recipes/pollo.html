Chicken in mango curry sauce

ingredients

  • Chicken breast ------- 500 gr
  • ground curry ---------- 5 gr
  • hot paprika ------------------- 2 gr
  • Fresh Ginger ---------- 5 gr
  • Sweet onion ---------- 1
  • Garlic cloves ---------------- 3
  • bay leaves ------------- 3
  • small cinnamon stick ----------------- 1
  • Yellow or black mustard seeds. --- 3gr
  • cumin grains ------------------ 3 gr
  • Ripe small mango ------------- 1
  • ground turmeric ------------------ 3 gr
  • Coconut milk or cream for cooking ------- 250 ml
  • ground black pepper
  • Salt
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • fresh parsley or cilantro

preparation

  1. Dry the chicken with kitchen paper and remove excess fat. Cut into small pieces and season. Heat a little olive oil in a good frying pan and add the chicken. Sauté over high heat until well browned. Add the curry spices and paprika, stir a few times, and remove. Peel the mango and crush the pulp.
  2. Blend the onion with the peeled ginger, the garlic cloves and a little water. Clean the pan from the chicken, heat a little oil and add the bay leaf, cinnamon, mustard seeds and cumin. Brown until they release their aroma, being careful not to burn yourself. Add the onion paste and cook, stirring occasionally, for about 12 to 15 minutes.
  3. Remove the bay leaf and cinnamon, add the crushed mango, turmeric and coconut milk or cream. Mix well and allow to reduce for a couple of minutes. Return the chicken, mix and season. Cover and cook for about 5 minutes. Uncover and allow the sauce to reduce for another 10 minutes over low heat.
