Chicken in mango curry sauce

ingredients

  1. Chicken breast ------- 500 gr
  2. ground curry ---------- 5 gr
  3. hot paprika ------------------- 2 gr
  4. Fresh Ginger ---------- 5 gr
  5. Sweet onion ---------- 1
  6. Garlic cloves ---------------- 3
  7. bay leaves ------------- 3
  8. small cinnamon stick ----------------- 1
  9. Yellow or black mustard seeds. --- 3gr
  10. cumin grains ------------------ 3 gr
  11. Ripe small mango ------------- 1
  12. ground turmeric ------------------ 3 gr
  13. Coconut milk or cream for cooking ------- 250 ml
  14. ground black pepper
  15. Salt
  16. Extra virgin olive oil
  17. fresh parsley or cilantro

preparation

Dry the chicken with kitchen paper and remove excess fat. Cut into small pieces and season. Heat a little olive oil in a good frying pan and add the chicken. Sauté over high heat until well browned. Add the curry spices and paprika, stir a few times, and remove. Peel the mango and crush the pulp.

Blend the onion with the peeled ginger, the garlic cloves and a little water. Clean the pan from the chicken, heat a little oil and add the bay leaf, cinnamon, mustard seeds and cumin. Brown until they release their aroma, being careful not to burn yourself. Add the onion paste and cook, stirring occasionally, for about 12 to 15 minutes.

Retirar la hoja de laurel y la canela, añadir el mango triturado, la cúrcuma y la leche o nata de coco. Mezclar bien y dejar reducir un par de minutos. Devolver el pollo, mezclar y sazonar. Tapar y cocinar durante unos 5 minutos. Destapa y deja que la salsa reduzca otros 10 minutos a fuego lento.
