Baked salmon with citrus and ginger sauce

ingredients

  1. Fresh salmon loins ------- 4
  2. Pink grapefruit ---------- 2
  3. Orange ------------------- 1
  4. Fresh Ginger ---------- 10 gr
  5. Maple syrup ---------- 2 cdas
  6. Butter ---------------- 20 gr
  7. salt and pepper

preparation

We will start by preheating the oven to 200ยบ with heat up and down. Place the cleaned and seasoned salmon loins in a baking dish lined with non-stick paper and reserve.

In a small saucepan, add the juice from one of the grapefruits and the orange juice, as well as the maple syrup and grated fresh ginger. We put everything to heat until it boils. Once it boils, lower the heat and cook for 15 minutes until a kind of syrup is formed. Off the heat add the butter and reserve.

We make some supreme of grapefruit segments, cutting it alive, without leaving any of the white part.

Bake the salmon for 15 minutes and serve with the citrus sauce and fresh grapefruit supreme.
